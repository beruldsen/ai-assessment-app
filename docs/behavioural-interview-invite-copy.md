# Behavioural Interview Invite Copy

Subject: Prepare for your behavioural interview

This interview works best when you can give clear, specific examples from your own experience.

Before you begin, please take a few minutes to think of 2 to 3 real customer situations you were personally involved in. Strong examples usually help you describe:

- the situation or customer context
- what you personally did
- why you chose that approach
- who you needed to influence
- what changed as a result

A few notes before you start:

- This is a thoughtful interview, not a quick survey.
- Strong answers are specific rather than general.
- Some examples may touch more than one capability area. If that happens, the interviewer may steer you toward the angle it needs to assess.
- There are no perfect answers. Honest, concrete examples are far more useful than polished but generic ones.

If possible, choose a time when you can focus without rushing.
